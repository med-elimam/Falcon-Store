# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- Perfume buyers in Nouakchott, Mauritania — mobile-first, Arabic-speaking (French secondary), ordering via WhatsApp and paying with Bankily/Masrvi/Sedad or cash on delivery.
- Two segments: (1) customers who want authentic niche/designer bottles (Creed, Xerjoff, Memo, Tiziana Terenzi, Unique'e Luxury), and (2) customers who want affordable 10ml decants filled from the original bottles.
- The store owner: needs a simple Arabic admin to edit prices, stock, availability, and manage orders. He will not tolerate friction.

## Product Purpose

E-commerce storefront for **Falcon Store — متجر الصقر للعطور**, a real perfume shop in Nouakchott. The site must convert visitors into WhatsApp orders. Success = the owner says "هذا متجري، لكن كأنه صار علامة عالمية" and customers complete local orders without confusion.

Key commercial angle: the 10ml decant offering ("جرّب الفخامة قبل اقتناء الزجاجة") is the strongest offer and gets a primary section, not a footnote.

## Brand Personality

Sharp · metallic · nocturnal. "THE SCENT VAULT" — a dark cinematic perfume vault with the falcon's edge. Obsidian black + Falcon crimson (#A20F35) carry the identity; gold is only a detail accent on luxury products. Trust is earned through documentation (authentic bottles, batch codes, clear availability), never claimed.

## Anti-references

- The royal black-and-gold perfume cliché — makes it one of a thousand identical stores.
- Generic Shopify templates; boring white stores; animated portfolio experiments.
- Ornate Arabic calligraphy fonts (reads cheap); excessive gold; excessive animation; slow intros; scroll hijacking; autoplay sound.
- American-style complex checkout — the flow is local: name, phone, area, payment method, WhatsApp confirmation.

## Design Principles

1. **Every animation sells** — motion must support navigation, product understanding, or purchase intent; never decoration for its own sake.
2. **Arabic-first, RTL-first** — Arabic is the primary UI language and layout direction; French/English are secondary and editorial.
3. **Trust through documentation** — show the bottle, the box, availability, concentration, origin; never claim what isn't documented.
4. **Local commerce reality** — MRU (new ouguiya) pricing only, WhatsApp ordering, Bankily/Masrvi/Sedad/COD, Nouakchott delivery.
5. **Fast on mobile** — the customer is on a phone in Nouakchott; performance beats spectacle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on every animation (crossfade or instant alternative).
- Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast on the dark surfaces (warm ivory #F3EFE8 on obsidian).
- Touch-first: large tap targets, no hover-only affordances, no custom cursor on mobile.
- Intro reveal capped at 1.5s and skippable; never blocks entry.
